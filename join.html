Membership Benefits

NP Memebership is for non profit organizations and there is no fee

This type of Membership is typically designed to help out some organization that are helping many in diffrent corner of the world, Although it's free it comes with great features to that are really useful to your organization. Since non profit organization are designed in a way that all the income goes back into it's operation this memebership help minimised some cost so that the income should be spent in something else.

Bronze Membership $500

As a Bronze Membership, you will enjoy some cool and amazing tools that comes with it, in that you will be offered some benefits like training which will help in the proper management of your organization.

Silver Membership $1500

Our silver membership is designed for users who already know the basics of the website, we offer full insights into our teams strategies throungh our educational ideas. The background and strategic thinking behind each setup is elucidated across diverse useful areas including: All of which you can use to hone your skills. We provide a weekly newsletter service guaranteeing you a full recap of weekly happenings both within and without Eight. Every month, the team of Eight will be doing a Q&A in which we answer all questions from our members.

Upon joining our platform as a silver member, you will receive an email with greetings and information enabling you to join our private Discord server.

Gold Membership $2000

As a Gold Member, you can join us at our private meetings. At these intimate meetings, we have local or specialized speakers to educate you on the hottest topics of the day. Nothing is ever sold or pitched at Gold Meetings. Plus, with a smaller group, it is easier for you to ask questions and receive personal attention. Keep on the cutting edge of rapidly changing news and strategies.

As a Gold Member, you are invited to have dinner with our featured speaker of the month at a private reception prior to, or after, our general meetings (subject to the Speaker's availability). These receptions are a wonderful opportunity for you to spend time “up close and personal” with our featured speaker and ask them questions. Unique networking experience!

As a Gold Member, you will receive reserve seating at our monthly meetings, if requested. Many of our Gold Members believe this is the best feature of Gold Membership, particularly on those nights when the event is sold out and there is standing room only.
